Welcome to Our Craft Brewery

We’re passionate about creating exceptional craft beers that celebrate tradition while embracing innovation. Every batch is brewed with care, using the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of the city, our brewery combines old-world craftsmanship with modern brewing science. We believe that great beer brings people together, and our taproom is a place where friends, family, and strangers become part of our brewing community.

From our signature IPAs to rich stouts and crisp lagers, each beer tells a story of dedication, creativity, and the pursuit of perfect flavor.

What We Brew

Hop-Forward Ales - Bold, aromatic, and bursting with flavor
Dark & Mysterious - Rich stouts and porters for the adventurous
Classic Lagers - Crisp, clean, and endlessly refreshing
Seasonal Specials - Limited releases that capture the moment

Visit our taproom to experience the full range of our handcrafted beers, or take home a growler to share with friends.

Visit Us

Our taproom is open seven days a week, featuring rotating taps, brewery tours, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Come taste the difference that passion and craftsmanship make.

Cheers to great beer and good company!

Copper Trail Amber Ale

Copper Trail Amber Ale

Style: American Amber Ale
ABV: 5.8%
IBU: 32

Tasting Notes

Our flagship amber ale strikes the perfect balance between rich malt character and hop complexity. Copper Trail pours a brilliant amber-copper color with a creamy off-white head.

Aroma: Toasted caramel, biscuit malt, with hints of citrus and pine from American hops

Flavor: Medium-bodied with layers of caramel sweetness, toffee, and bread crust. A gentle hop bitterness provides balance without overwhelming the malt profile.
